Karnataka is a state in the southwestern region of India. It is the eighth largest state by population and the ninth largest by area. Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of Karnataka and is one of the largest cities in India. Karnataka is known for its rich cultural heritage, including historic sites like Hampi and the Mysore Palace, as well as its scenic beauty, such as the Western Ghats mountain range and the beaches of Udupi and Gokarna.
