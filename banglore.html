About Bangalore

Bangalore, also known as Bengaluru, is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is often referred to as the Silicon Valley of India due to its thriving technology industry. Bangalore is also known for its pleasant climate, beautiful parks and gardens, and diverse cultural heritage.

History

Bangalore has a rich history that dates back to the 16th century. The city was a stronghold of several South Indian dynasties, including the Vijayanagara Empire and the Keladi Nayaka Kingdom. In the 18th century, Bangalore became a part of the British East India Company, and it was during this time that the city began to grow and develop as a modern city.
